Portugal's Forests: A Visual Exploration

Our application offers a unique, interactive platform for exploring comprehensive data related to the health and commercial aspects of forests of Portugal.


The developed visualizations aim to provide insightful analyses and facilitate a deeper understanding of the dynamic nature of these biomes. By leveraging data from PORDATA Portugal Floresta-474, we provide information that includes aspects like wildfire occurrences, areas affected, and commercial activities involving forest products.


Whether you are a researcher, educator, policy maker, or simply an enthusiast of environmental studies, our application is designed to cater to your curiosity and informational needs. Explore interactive charts that shed light on the past, present, and future of Portugal's forested regions. Engage with our content and embark on a journey of discovery and learning about one of Portugal's most precious natural resources.


This project was developed within the scope of the Information Visualization course in 2023.
